Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Introduction

Quiet Summer LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Life Tab mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect your photos, videos, or other media content. Your photos and videos remain stored only on your device. We never transmit, upload, or have access to the actual image or video files you add to your daily logs.

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Activity Data: Activity categories and labels you create to organize your entries (stored locally on your device).

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including features used and session duration, to improve our service.
  • Location Data: Only when you explicitly grant permission, we may access your location to tag entries with a place. You can revoke location access at any time in your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and functionality
  • Respond to your requests, comments, or questions
  • Send you important updates about the App (such as security notices)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Your photos, videos, and journal entries are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. We do not access, view, or analyze the content of your media.

Data Storage and Security

Your photos and media stay on your device. The App stores your photos, videos, and journal entries locally on your device only. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photo or video content to our servers or any third party.

For any account-related data we do collect (such as email), we use industry-standard encryption and security practices. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

Your photos and content remain private on your device and are not accessible to us or other users unless you explicitly choose to share them.

This does not affect your use of your own cloud storage (such as iCloud or Google Photos). How you back up or sync your photos is entirely up to you—we have no access to your personal cloud accounts.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the App (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (we do not store your photos or media).
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Your photos remain on your device; you can delete them from your device at any time.
  • Export: Your photos and media are already on your device—you have full control. You may request a copy of any account data we hold.
  • Opt-Out: Disable optional data collection (location, analytics) in your device or app settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@quietsummerllc.com.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store (distribution and payments)

Note: We do not use any cloud storage or analytics services that would have access to your photos or media content. This refers only to our systems—it does not restrict or affect your use of your own personal cloud storage (such as iCloud or Google Photos), which is entirely under your control.

Data Retention

Your photos and journal content are stored only on your device—we do not retain them. For any account-related data we do collect, we retain it for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
